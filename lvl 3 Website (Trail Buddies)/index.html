About Trail Buddies

What is trail buddies?

Hi everyone. i've created this group to give members a chance to find riding buddies. I have friends who live close to me but often find I'm not available to ride when they are and vice versa they're not available when I am , this means I end up going riding on my own. My hope is that this community can be used by riders to find and meet up with people to go riding with either on a regular basis or for a one off ride. Maybe you're planning a ride in an area you haven't been to before and tagging along with someone or a group who know the trails would be an advantage. Feel free to ask for and offer advice. We are all riders with different experience levels and abilities. There should be a wealth of knowledge of riding and mechanical skills , as well as hopefully trail routes , let's use those to benefit each other.
